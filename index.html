Connecting Biotechnology and Quantum Computing.

Quantum Computing has the potential to change the border between what is efficiently solvable and what is effectively intractable. Hopefully Coherent intends to rigorously map out the consequences of quantum computing for biotechnology. The topics covered thusfar are:
  • Lattice Proteins
  • Ligand Binding
  • Multiple Sequence Alignment
  • Sequence Assembly
  • De Novo Assembly
  • Machine Learning
